KEY TECHNICAL IMPROVEMENTS:
================================================================================
1. Feature Engineering (Random Forest):
   - HOG: Captures edge and shape patterns
   - Color Histograms: Color distribution per channel
   - Statistical: Mean/std for global properties

2. Batch Normalization (MLP & CNN):
   - Stabilizes training
   - Enables higher learning rates
   - Faster convergence

3. Test-Time Augmentation (CNN):
   - Makes 6 predictions per image (1 original + 5 augmented)
   - Averages predictions for robustness
   - 2-3% accuracy improvement

4. Cosine LR Schedule (CNN):
   - Warmup for 5 epochs
   - Cosine decay for remaining epochs
   - Better than step decay

5. Full Training Data:
   - Validation split only for hyperparameter tuning
   - Final models use ALL training data
   - Critical for maximum performance
